About Pueblo

Pueblo Cannabis Co. was founded to create a warm, community-centered destination where cannabis, art, and culture intersect.

Inspired by desert aesthetics and local creativity, Pueblo gives independent artists and wellness-focused cannabis consumers a place to connect.

Whether you're discovering a new strain, attending an art opening, or supporting local creators, Pueblo is built for the people of the community.
